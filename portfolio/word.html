graphic wit

The goal with this was to incorporate graphic wit into design. After exploring different everyday objects, I drew a clothespin in Illustrator and then brainstormed different words I could combine with it. I landed on the word ‘abandonment’, with the objective of communicating a sage observation and a visual twist. I focused on the concept of being “hung out to dry”.

phantom pizza

Working on logo development, I created the identity for ‘Phantom Pizza’. I wanted the design to translate well onto different mediums and to communicate both the ‘phantom’ and ‘pizza’ part of the name. The goal was to keep it fun and lighthearted, while also communicating professionalism.
